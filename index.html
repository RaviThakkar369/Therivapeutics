Mission Statement

Research and development of innovative therapeutic solutions, providing scientific services including in silico design, screening, testing, and consulting; collaborating with for-profit and non-profit organizations to accelerate discovery and development in the biotechnology sector.

Meet the founder & CEO - Ravindra Thakkar

Ravindra Thakkar is the Founder and CEO of Therivapeutics LLC, bringing over a decade of experience in exploring the microscopic world of cells, organelles, and biomolecules.

His work integrates experimental and computational approaches in structural biology, with a focus on computational biomolecular modeling and electron microscopy for therapeutic applications.

He has investigated cellular changes in disease, designed and patented therapeutic peptides, and collaborated across disciplines to drive biomedical innovation.

He is passionate about learning new technologies and building partnerships to tackle complex challenges that improve human health and benefit society.
