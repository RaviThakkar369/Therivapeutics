Mission Statement

About Me

I’m Ravi Thakkar, a scientist and entrepreneur with experience exploring the microscopic world of cells, organelles, and biomolecules. My work bridges experimental and computational approaches in structural biology, specializing in cryo-electron microscopy and protein design for therapeutic applications. I’ve studied cellular changes in disease, designed and patented therapeutic peptides, and collaborated across disciplines to advance biomedical innovation. I enjoy learning new tools and partnering with others to help solve complex challenges for the betterment of health and society.
